Here I share some gym equipment pictures for more references with gym equipment information:

Squat Power Rack:

Affordable and occupies less space compared to a full rack. It is usually meant to help with squats and bench press but can also be used for various other exercises like shoulder press and push press.

Barbell:

One of the most versatile pieces of equipment in any gym. It helps maintain good posture and prevent body swaying during exercises. Suitable for various exercises.

Weight Bench Machine:

A versatile must-have for any gym. Usually adjustable with three angles: incline, flat, and decline. It can be used for many exercises.

Leg Press Machine:

Works on the quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves simultaneously. Comes with a backrest, leg press, and adjustable seat. Correct exercise involves bending and extending the legs slowly.

Leg Curl and Leg Extension:

Targets glutes, hamstrings, and quads. This dual-purpose machine helps in saving cost and space.

Dumbbells:

Used individually or in pairs. Handles are made of steel or iron, and weights/weight plates can be steel, iron, rubber, or cement. Usually adjustable.
